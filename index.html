Our Love Story

How We Met

On a quiet February evening in 2020, two hearts connected through the digital world of Facebook. What started as simple messages soon blossomed into something magical. We found ourselves sharing our deepest insecurities, our hidden vulnerabilities, and our dreams with each other. Without realizing it, we were building a bridge of trust and understanding. Day by day, conversation by conversation, we grew closer, creating an unbreakable bond that would change our lives forever.

February 22, 2020

First Family Visit

After months of virtual connection and growing love, the moment finally arrived when we decided to take the next big step. With nervous excitement and hopeful hearts, we introduced each other to our families. What began as a formal meeting soon transformed into warm embraces and genuine acceptance. Seeing the joy in our parents' eyes and feeling their blessings made us realize that our love had found its home not just in each other's hearts, but in our united families too.

July 2022

First Mountain Together

Hand in hand, we embarked on our first mountain adventure together, climbing not just the peaks but also deeper into each other's hearts. With every step upward, we discovered new strengths in ourselves and each other. The crisp mountain air, the breathtaking views, and the shared challenge brought us closer than ever. Standing at the summit together, we realized that no mountain was too high when we had each other's support and love.

May 2023

First Ocean Together

The endless blue horizon mirrored the boundless love we felt as we stood together by the ocean for the first time. The rhythmic waves seemed to whisper promises of our future, while the warm sand beneath our feet reminded us to stay grounded in our love. We walked along the shore, collecting shells and memories, knowing that just like the eternal ocean, our love would be timeless and infinite.

August 2023
